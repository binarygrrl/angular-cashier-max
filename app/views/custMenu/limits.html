Limits

Set Limits

Limits control the maximum amount spent for wagers, losses, deposits and withdrawals. If there is a system imposed limit on a category, your personal limit cannot be set on that category to be higher than the system's currently imposed limit.

For example, if the system has a limit on withdrawals to be $1,000/day, you cannot set your personal limit to more than $1,000. Once your personal limits are set, they can be changed on request, but there is a 24 hour waiting period after the request is made.
